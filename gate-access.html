Gate & Lakehouse Access

Information on obtaining gate remotes, key fobs, and access codes for the community gate and lakehouse.

How to Request Access

To request a remote, key fob, or code, please contact the Community Manager with your name, address, and desired access type. Requests may require proof of residency and payment for replacements.

Contact: Manager@RaviniaVillasHOA.org

Gate Remotes

What: Hand-held remotes for vehicle entry.

Cost: New remotes may incur a replacement fee (refer to the Manager for current pricing).

How to get one: Submit a request to the Manager including your address and vehicle info. Remote programming is done by the vendor or Manager during scheduled visits.

Gate Key Fobs

What: Key fobs for pedestrian gate entry or alternate access.

Cost: Replacement fees may apply.

How to get one: Contact the Manager with your request. Provide proof of residency and the name on the property record if requested.

Gate Access Codes

What: Temporary or permanent PIN codes used for gate entry.

When used: Short-term guests, service providers, or meeting access.

How to request: Requests for temporary codes should include the date/time window and reason (e.g., contractor visit). Permanent codes are issued sparingly and under Board/Manager approval.

Vendor/Service Provider Access

For lawn crews, pool service, contractors, or deliveries, request a temporary code or scheduling so staff can enter during approved service windows.

Lost / Stolen Remotes or Fobs

Report immediately to the Manager. Lost or stolen devices should be deactivated and replaced; a replacement fee may apply to cover reprogramming and device cost.

Rules & Security

Access devices and codes are for residents only. Do not share permanent codes publicly. The HOA may reset codes for security reasons. Unauthorized duplication or distribution may lead to revocation of privileges.

Office Hours / Pickup

Device pickup or programming is by appointment only. Please contact the Manager to schedule.

FAQ

  • How long for a replacement? Typically processed within 3–14 business days depending on availability and vendor scheduling.
  • Can guests use my code? Temporary codes may be issued for specific visits; permanent codes should not be shared.
  • Is there a master code? The HOA maintains administrative codes; residents should not request or attempt to use admin credentials.

Questions or Requests

Send an email with your name, address, and request details to: Manager@RaviniaVillasHOA.org
