Vantaca Access — Quick Instructions

If you already have an account

1. Use Send Log In Code (Recommended)

This is the easiest method — no password required.

  1. Enter the email address you used to create your account.
  2. Click “Send Log In Code”.
  3. Check your email for a message from Inframark. (Check spam/junk if needed.)
  4. Enter the verification code to complete login.

Tip: The email usually arrives within seconds but may take a few minutes.

2. Log in with your password

  1. Click “Log In With Password”.
  2. Enter your email address and password.

3. Forgot or need to reset your password

  1. Click “Log In With Password”.
  2. Select “Forgot your password?”.
  3. Enter your email and click “Send Code”.
  4. Check your email and follow the reset instructions.

If you do NOT have an account

  1. Click “Log In With Password”, then select “Sign Up”.
  2. If you have an Account Number and Registration Key, enter them and submit.
  3. If you don’t have those details, select “Don’t have an account number or registration key?” and complete the request form.
  4. Include your Account Number if available; otherwise leave it blank.

Inframark will contact you with additional instructions if needed.

Need help?

Contact Inframark support using the phone number or email provided by the community. When contacting support, include your full name and the email address used during registration.
