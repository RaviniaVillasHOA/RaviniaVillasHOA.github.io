Architectural Review Committee

Committee Charter

Architectural Review Committee & Homeowner Guidelines includes:

  • The committee’s charter
  • Detailed guidelines for homeowners
  • Supporting materials, including:
  • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
  • Standards for:
    • Outdoor landscaping and lighting
    • Driveway pavers
    • Garage door screens
    • Hurricane window and door protection
    • Security cameras and doorbell cameras
  • Referenced definitions
  • The community plant list
  • Pre-approved repairs and modifications
  • An example of a complete Certificate of Insurance for reference

This document is essential reading before submitting an ARC application.
