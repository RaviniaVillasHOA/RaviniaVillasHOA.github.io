Architectural Review Committee

DRAFT Committee Charter

1. Purpose

The purpose of the ARC is to preserve and enhance the aesthetic harmony, architectural integrity, and landscaping standards of the community.

2. Authority

This Architectural Review Committee ("ARC") is established by the Board of Directors of the Association pursuant to Florida Statute §720.3035 and the governing documents of the Association. The ARC is authorized to review and approve architectural and landscaping improvements as provided in the Declaration of Covenants, Conditions, and Restrictions.

3. Composition and Appointment

The ARC shall consist of at least three (3) members appointed by the Board of Directors. Members may be Directors or non-Directors unless restricted by the governing documents.

4. Scope of Authority

The ARC shall review applications for exterior and visible improvements, including but not limited to structures, fences, paint colors, roofs, driveways, patios, lighting, signage, and all landscaping-related changes such as plantings, removals, tree work, irrigation modifications, hardscape features, and landscape edging. The ARC has no authority to impose fines or penalties under Florida Statute §720.305.

5. Procedures

Owners must submit written applications with sufficient plans, drawings, plant lists, and specifications. The ARC shall issue a written decision within the timeframe established in the governing documents or if none is stated, within a reasonable time as required by Florida law.

6. Standards of Review

Decisions shall be based on objective architectural and landscaping standards set forth in the governing documents and applied uniformly, reasonably, and in good faith.

7. Appeals

An Owner may appeal an ARC decision to the Board of Directors in accordance with the Association’s governing documents. The Board’s decision shall be final.

8. Records

ARC records, including landscaping approvals, denials, and conditions, shall be maintained as official Association records pursuant to Florida Statute §720.303(4).

9. Indemnification

ARC members shall be indemnified by the Association to the fullest extent permitted by Florida law for acts performed in good faith within the scope of their authority.

10. Charter Review and Amendment

This Charter may be reviewed periodically and amended by the Board of Directors to remain consistent with governing documents and community needs.
